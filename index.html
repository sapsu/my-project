Blood pressure chart

Here you can enter your blood pressure measurement data and obtain a calculated average blood pressure level reading, accompanied with personalized insights according to the result.

For a reliable estimate of your average blood pressure, it is recommended that you follow these guidelines:

  • Measure your blood pressure in a calm surrounding, after at least 10-15 minutes of rest (sitting or lying down).
  • Measure the blood pressure on 5 different days.
  • If you have been prescribed medication for high blood pressure, you should take it normally during the days you do these measurements.
  • Make sure your blood pressure meter is functioning properly.
